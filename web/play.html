HOW TO PLAY
BASICS
Conquer a 10x10 ocean grid by deploying crab troops, attacking enemies, and capturing territory. First to hold 60 spots wins!
COMBAT (RISK DICE)
When you attack, both sides roll dice (1-6). Highest dice are compared pair by pair. Higher roll wins — loser removes 1 troop. Ties go to the defender.
Attacker diceUp to 3 (must leave 1 behind)
Defender diceUp to 2
QTE bonusTap in sweet spot = +1 to best die
Tip: Attack with 3+ troops for 3 dice. Defending with 2 troops gives you 2 dice. Numbers matter!
TERRAIN EFFECTS
Shore ~Neutral — 1x shells
Reef ^Defender +1 — 1.5x shells
Deep Water =Attacker -1 — 1x shells
Coral *+1 def per adj coral — 2x shells
Crown +No combat bonus — 3x shells
Tip: Reef and coral spots are much easier to defend. Deep water is hard to invade.

Every 10 seconds, each spot you hold generates shells based on terrain.
